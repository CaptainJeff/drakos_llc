Peter Drakos

203-569-7679, 203-569-7679, pdrakos@drakosllc.com

Work Experience:

Peter G. Drakos is a maritime lawyer admitted to practice in the states of New York and Connecticut. He practiced with the law firms of Cardillo & Corbett (1983 – 2000), Healy & Baillie LLP (2001 – 2007), and Blank Rome LLP (2007 – 2008). In 2008 he withdrew from Blank Rome to become President of Coastal Connect LLC, a start-up company in the short sea shipping sector, working towards launching a Ro-Ro service in New England. The financial crisis of 2008 put the Ro-Ro project on hold, and in 2009 he returned to the practice of law with his own firm, Peter G. Drakos, LLC.

During his 30 years of practice in the field of maritime law he has concentrated in the areas of charter party and bill of lading law, in rem procedures, ship sale and purchase, ship finance and ship registration. He is a member of the Maritime Law Association of the United States; a past Vice-Chairman of the Admiralty and Maritime Law Committee of the American Bar Association (International Law Section); a past President of the Connecticut Maritime Association; an Adjunct Professor in the Maritime Studies Department at the University of Connecticut -Avery Point, teaching commercial shipping; and a member of the Board of Trustees of the Sea Research Foundation/Mystic Aquarium. He graduated from the University of Vermont in 1978, and Gonzaga University School of Law, cum laude, in 1983.
